Never skip oats breakfast. It is the foundation of your entire day and energy levels.
⚡
5g creatine every single day — including Sunday. Consistency is what makes it work.
🚶
10,000 steps daily. This burns belly fat more than any gym exercise. Non-negotiable.

Zero junk, zero sugar, zero fried food on weekdays. Sunday = 1 cheat meal max.

Stop eating by 9:00 PM. Fasting from 9PM–8AM = 11hr fat-burning window.

Mind-muscle connection over ego lifting. Feel every rep. Quality over quantity always.
